Introduction

A key tenant of our approach is a focus on the operational aspect of technology. As organizations continue to invest in transformative solutions powered by new and powerful technology, there is an increased burden on Ops to be able to install, configure, manage, monitor, and support these platforms.

Inspired by the Checklist Manifesto by Atul Gawande, Eucalyptus was designed to be able to create, store, and manage tasks. We believe there is a direct correlation between simplicity and stability, and by implementing FlightPlans we can make these complex processes much simpler. By leveraging Eucalyptus, we increase consistency while providing education, logs, and enabling collaboration.
